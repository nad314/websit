GoldenPine Luxury Honey
52€ , 280g (9.9oz)
GoldenPine Luxury is the ecologic meadow honey coming in 4x70g (2.5oz) jars, produced naturally in a non-industrial environment. GoldenPine Luxury meadow honey is mild flavoured, softly yellow colored and has a crystal texture due to 23 karat gold it contains.
GoldenPine Luxury Honey is created by bees that collect pollen from meadow plants blooming in summer. Nectar collected from the flowers is the main source of carbohydrates produced into easily digestible sweetness - fructose and glucose, which are the main ingredients of Golden Pine Luxury Honey.

GoldenPine Chestnut Honey
20€, 320g (11oz)
GoldenPine Chestnut Honey, also known as "winter honey", is recognized by its red-brown color and intense flavour. It is often used as an ingredient for cakes and as a supplement in tea and cookies, instead of sugar.
GoldenPine Chestnut Honey is created by bee hives situated in pure chestnut forests. Bees gather chestnut pollen which blooms in early June and produce honey that contains 85% of the pollen. Due to its high purity it is often compared to Cabernet Sauvignon wine. GoldenPine Chestnut honey is a great way to start making your diet healthier.
